NUTRITION IMAGE ANALYSIS

Food is essential for human life and has been the concern of many healthcare conventions. Nowadays new dietary assessment and nutrition analysis tools enable more opportunities to help people understand their daily eating habits, exploring nutrition patterns and maintain a health diet. Nutritional analysis is the process of determining the nutrtional content of food. It is a vital part of analytics chemistry that provides information about the chemical compostion, processing, quality control and contamination of food. It ensures complicance with trade and food laws.
